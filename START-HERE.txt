WOLF EQUIPMENT CATALOGUE
========================

1. Read README.md.
2. Keep your raw Current RMS export in:
   private-import/current-products.csv
3. Never commit that raw CSV to public GitHub.
4. Double-click “Update Catalogue.command” to rebuild the catalogue and import fresh images.
5. Double-click “Preview Catalogue.command” to preview it locally.
6. Commit and push the generated equipment folder, data controls and reports.

The sample build already contains 191 generated product pages for testing.
