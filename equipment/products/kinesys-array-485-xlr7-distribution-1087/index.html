Kinesys Array 485 - XLR7 Distribution

Array 485 is a 1 in 8 out RS485 distribution unit for use with Elevation1+.

The rear panel features 1 in 8 out XLR7 data connections for outputting RS485 data to Elevation1+ units. The front panel offers link, data and emergency stop status indicators as well as the facility to plug a Rigger remote in for local hoist control.
