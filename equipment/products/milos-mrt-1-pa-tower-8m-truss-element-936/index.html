Milos MRT 1 PA Tower - 8m - Truss Element

Description
The MRT1 is our smallest rigging tower. Its size and weight makes it the ideal choice for small events, bands, and touring promotional road shows. It incorporates our M290V truss system for durability and safety. Call or e-mail us today for more information or to receive a quote.

Key benefits
Compact heavy-duty M290 series Line Array tower solution
8m (26.25 ft) max. height - 750 Kg SWL
Hinge mast & optimised components for a fast assembly
Optional tower helper erecting frame available
Electric chain hoist or manual chain block connection point
Screwjack feet for quick & easy levelling
EXE hoists recommended
Powder coat colour finish available on request
