RIEDEL PUNQTUM Q210 PW SPEAKER STATION

Designed to support the free PunQtum Wireless app, the Q210 PW Speaker Station gives you full control in an affordable, easy-to-use package – it’s a self-contained communications hub for wired and wireless communication. As well as the four wireless connections, the station is also capable of supporting up to 4 wired beltpacks over PoE. The Q210 PW speaker station is equipped with 4-channels itself and can support various additional functions.
