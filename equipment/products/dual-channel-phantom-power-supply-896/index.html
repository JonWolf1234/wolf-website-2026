Dual channel phantom power supply

The PH-PSU DUAL is a dual channel phantom power supply with the option of using either a 12V DC adaptor or 2x 9V batteries. The PH-PSU DUAL also has selectable 12V/48V phantom power, making this unit both versatile and portable.

2x Female XLR inputs
2x Male XLR outputs
Phantom power: 12V/48V switchable
Power: 2x 9V batteries (included) or an optional 12VDC adaptor (PW03058)
