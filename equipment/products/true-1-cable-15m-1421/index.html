True 1 Cable - 15m

Rugged power link cables manufactured using genuine Neutrik powerCON TRUE1 TOP connectors fitted to 1.5mm² Titanex H07RN-F tough rubber sheathed cable. The powerCON TRUE1 TOP is a locking 16A true mains connector for indoor or outdoor applications. It replaces appliance couplers wherever a very rugged solution in combination with a locking device is needed in order to guarantee a safe power connection.
