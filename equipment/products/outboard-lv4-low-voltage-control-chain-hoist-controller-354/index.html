Outboard LV4 Low Voltage Control Chain Hoist Controller

The Outboard LV4 Low Voltage Control Chain Hoist Controller is a four way hoist controller:

Four channels of low-voltage control and 3-phase hoist power distro
Selectable Normal multichannel mode or Pickle mode
Industry standard rear panel C-Form control and power connectors
Input voltage phase indicators and reversal switch
Supplied with RC4 handset for remote control, with built-in Emergency Stop and 10m extension
Integral Emergency Stop button, link-able across multiple units. E-Stop circuit features automatic self-test function
Fully compliant with CE directives
