RIEDEL PUNQTUM Q925 DUAL EAR HEADSET

The PunQtum Q925 headsets have been designed to elevate the customer experience, delivering lightweight and comfortable wear combined with advanced audio technology. The PunQtum Q915 is a one-ear headset and the PunQtum Q925 is its dual-ear counterpart. Optimised for speech, the headset’s dynamic microphones filter out environmental noise, leading to a disruption-free listening experience. Coupled with an adjustable boom that’s wearable on either side of the device, the Q915 and Q925 are comfortable and flexible headsets designed to deliver seamless communication.
