RIEDEL PUNQTUM Q110 BELT PACK

The easy-to-use and ergonomic Q110 Beltpack offers two channels of partyline intercom and is packed with many useful features. Power can be supplied via a standard PoE switch or from the PunQtum speaker station. Beltpacks are also daisy-chainable, allowing up to eight units to be connected on a single network connection – you can even replay missed messages like voicemail.

KEY FEATURES

Plug and Play
Powered by any PoE source (Switch/Injector)
Daisy-chain an unlimited number of beltpacks (dependent on PoE source)
Audio replay of missed calls (up to 30 seconds)
No master station needed
Supports industry standard headsets (4-Pin)
Works with any network switch
Talk/Listen to any 2 out of 32 partylines
2 configurable “Talk-to-many“ channels
Flexible configuration
Choose 1 out of 4 program inputs*
Operate 4 out of 32 GPIO outputs*
Talk to max. 4 PA channels*
On- and Offline configuration software (Win/Mac)
