2 cell DWE Molefay - Black

Commonly known as 'audience blinders', This 2 cell Molefays contains one circuit of Par 36 lamps.
Designed with ease of use in mind, they feature robust lightweight aluminium construction, semi-gloss black electrostatic paint finish, heavy duty yoke with positive lock off, using a tee bar to enable unit to be set at any desired angle. In addition, each lamp bank will pan independently to alter light effect.
Lamp change itself is simplicity due to quick release knobs on lamp retaining ring which are chrome plated and are fitted with a safety mesh as standard.
